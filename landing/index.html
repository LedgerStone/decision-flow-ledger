Process

How it works

Every decision follows a four-step auditable pipeline. No shortcuts, no exceptions.

1

Submit

An operator submits a query with a reason. The request is hashed, timestamped, and written to the immutable ledger.

2

Approve

Two independent approvers must sign off. Each approval is a separate ledger entry with its own cryptographic hash.

3

Execute

Only after dual approval can the query be executed. The executor, timestamp, and result hash are permanently recorded.

4

Blockchain

Every event is mined into a proof-of-work blockchain. Cross-verification ensures PostgreSQL and blockchain never diverge.

Benefits

Why AIP-X

Built for organizations where "trust us" isn't enough.

Immutable audit trail

Every action is recorded in a hash-chain ledger protected by database triggers that prevent any UPDATE or DELETE. A parallel blockchain provides independent verification. Tampering with one requires breaking both — which is computationally infeasible.

SHA-256 hash chain Merkle trees Proof of work

Full AI decision traceability

Know exactly who requested what, who approved it, and when it was executed. Every decision carries a documented reason, named operators, and a tamper-proof timeline. Auditors can reconstruct the entire decision history in seconds.

Who, what, when, why Timestamped events Cross-verification

Multi-operator approval

The multi-signature workflow ensures no single person can access sensitive data without oversight. Role-based access control with supervisor, judge, and analyst roles provides defense in depth. Full accountability by design, not by policy.

2-of-N signatures Role-based access Separation of duties
